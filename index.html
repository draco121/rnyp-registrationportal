What is Ranbhoomi?

We are bringing particioants from Different institution across the country to debate with and against the participants from our city. Our is aimed to help youth overcome the state of unpreparedness to meet the challenges of present scenario. We wish to deliver to the city the recognition is always longed for.


What is Youth Parliament?

Youth Parliament, in simplest terms is a simulation of the actual parliament. In a youth parliament, students from various schools, colleges and universities participate and are allocated constituencies. They have to represent their constituency by characterizing the MP who is the representative in the actual parliament. There is a speaker and a deputy speaker who regulates the process of debates and discussions. It is usually a two day event.
